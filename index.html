About SimpleOcean

SimpleOcean is an enviromental website that provides information about the current state of ocean and the environmental harms that it currently faces. The main purpose of the website is to be a fun, but also an informative guide on damaged ocean-realted topics such as corals, beaches, and ocean ecosystems. It will contain information on how to help the ocean and the environment, as well as tips on how to be more sustainable.

Recent Effects

The ocean has experienced sudden changes and can be mostly notably seen in its beaches, corals, and ecosystems.

Negative Causes

One of the most common causes of ocean pollution is plastic. Plastic is a common material that is used in many products around the globe.

Impacted Places

Places such as Australia and Florida are experiencing mass bleaching resulting in the endangerment to the coral reef ecosystems
